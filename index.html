PAPER SUBMISSION

The conferences proceedings will be published in the book series of Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering by Springer. Normally, papers in this series are indexed by EI and Scopus. If a paper is not presented in the conference, the paper will be excluded from the conference proceedings.

Please note that the page length limit is 8-10 pages. Extra pages will incur extra page charges. Each paper is allowed to have maximum 2 extra pages with raising JPY 15,000 registration fee per extra page.

Please follow the following templates of Springer book series in the preparation of your manuscripts.

  • LaTeX: LaTeX Package.zip
  • Microsoft Word: Word Template.zip

Please submit your manuscript via https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICGEC2025

Guidelines for paper submission can be downloaded here.

Dear Prospective Organizers,

Thank you for considering this Call for Invited Sessions from ICGEC-2025. ICGEC-2025 has the highest respect for organizers of Invited Sessions. KEYNOTE SPEECH

Zhenyu Meng is a Level-III Professor and Vice Dean of the School of Computer Science and Mathematics at Fujian University of Technology, and the Director of Institute of Artificial Intelligence at the University. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Application Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, and he serves as the Deputy Director of the Computational Intelligence and Its Applications Committee of the Chinese Association of Automation (CAA), a peer-review expert for the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), a dissertation review expert for the Academic Degrees Center of the Ministry of Education, a Category-B High-Level Talent of Fujian Province, and a review expert for provincial key programs as well as talent programs in Fujian Province and in Xiamen City & Fouzhou City. He is also a review expert for faculty title promotions in Fujian universities and Academic Bridge, a Senior Member of IEEE and CCF, a lifetime member of CAA, a member of ACM, a committee member of CCF's Technical Committees on Artificial Intelligence and Pattern Recognition, and a member of the expert databases for Shenzhen Science and Technology Projects and Fuzhou Scientific and Technological Achievements Transformation. He has led eight projects including the NSFC fundation and other projects, He has also participated in ten major projects, including national NDRC demonstration projects, subprojects of the National 863 Program, and high-level talent projects in Shenzhen.

He has published more than 50 SCI/EI papers as first author/corresponding author, including over 40 papers in CAS Q1 SCI journals. His PaDE algorithm published in KBS in 2019 became a Highly Cited Paper in Web of Science in November of the same year and remains highly cited. His HARD-DE algorithm, published in 2019, received a letter of commendation from Prof. Adam Piotrowski—Associate Editor of the CAS Q1 journal SWEVO and a researcher at the Polish Academy of Sciences—recommending it as the best algorithm for solving the air–water model and ranking it first among 12 state-of-the-art algorithms. The study recommending the HARD-DE algorithm was published in June 2021 in the CAS Q1 journal Journal of Hydrology (see: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0022169421002663). Additionally, he was listed in Stanford University Professor John P. A. Ioannidis and Elsevier's "World's Top 2% Scientists" rankings for Annual Scientific Impact in 2021, 2023, 2024, and 2025 (see: https://elsevier.digitalcommonsdata.com/datasets/btchxktzyw/).

He holds six authorized patents as the first inventor and has received two international silver medals in invention competitions (Geneva International Invention Exhibition and Nuremberg International Invention Exhibition). He serves as a reviewer for multiple WoS JCR Q1 journals, including IEEE-TEVC, TKDE, TIFS, TSMCA, TCYB, TNNLS, TII, TEC, INS, KBS, SWEVO, ASOC, ESWA, EAAI, Renewable Energy, Chaos, Solitons & Fractals, ISA Transactions, Journal of the Franklin Institute, as well as Chinese SCI journals such as Chinese Journal of Electronics, IEEE/CAA journal of Automatica Sinica.
